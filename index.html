A reference for teams to quickly find answers to common questions using AI-powered search across our SharePoint documents.

Questions answered directly from your SharePoint files

Ask Bulfinch can search and synthesize information from lease documents, loan agreements, budget packages, valuation files, capital plans, and more — all stored in SharePoint. The prompts below are ready to use. Replace anything in [brackets] with the specific property, tenant, or year you need.

Tips for best results

  • Be specific. Include the full property address or tenant name — "60 First Ave, Waltham" works better than "the Waltham property."
  • Add a time period. For budget or valuation questions, specify the year or quarter — "2026 budget" or "Q4 2025."
  • Ask to summarize. For long documents like loan agreements, add "summarize" or "focus on [topic]" to get a direct answer rather than raw text.
  • Chain questions. After getting an answer, follow up — "What notice period applies?" or "How does this compare to last year?"

Asset Management

Valuation & Performance
What is a property currently valued at and what assumptions drove that value?
"What is the current internal valuation of [property] and what assumptions were used — discount rate, exit cap rate, and market rent?"

Pulls from the Quarterly Valuation Tracker. Returns the current value, prior quarter comparison, and the methodology notes written by the AM team.

How has a property's value trended over the past year?
"How has the valuation of [property] changed over the last four quarters? Summarize the key drivers of any changes."

The Valuation Tracker includes prior quarter values and methodology notes going back multiple years, so quarter-over-quarter changes and the reasons behind them are readily available.

What is a property's debt yield and DSCR?
"What is the debt yield and DSCR at [property] as of the most recent reporting period?"

Found in loan compliance reporting and budget packages.

What is a property's NOI for a given year?
"What is the NOI for [property] for [year], and how does it compare to the prior year?"

Pulls from budget packages and the AM deck presentation backup files.

Leasing & Tenancy
Does a tenant have renewal, termination, or expansion options?
"Does [tenant] have any renewal options, termination options, or expansion rights at [property]? Summarize the terms."

Searches the lease and all amendments for the tenant. Returns notice periods, conditions, pricing, and any other relevant terms.

What does the portfolio lease rollover schedule look like?
"What is the portfolio lease rollover schedule for the next 24 months based on the most recent Asset Management meeting presentation?"

The AM meeting decks include a 10-year rollover chart broken out by year as a percentage of portfolio. Best source for portfolio-wide expiration exposure.

What are the key leasing updates across the portfolio?
"What are the key leasing updates and market observations from the most recent Asset Management meeting presentation?"

AM meeting decks are updated regularly and include new leases, renewals, active proposals, and market commentary across the portfolio.

Debt & Capital
What are the loan terms for a property?
"What are the loan terms for [property] — lender, balance, rate, maturity, and any extension options?"

Loan agreements are consistently filed under each property's debt folder and are fully readable. Returns a clean summary of all key terms.

What are the extension conditions for a specific loan?
"What are the extension conditions for the loan at [property]? Include notice periods, financial tests, and any fees required."

Extension provisions are typically in Section 2.4 of loan agreements. Returns LTV thresholds, debt yield and DSCR requirements, notice windows, and extension fee obligations.

Which properties have debt maturing soon?
"Which properties have debt maturing in the next 18 months? Summarize from the most recent Asset Management meeting or Leviathan presentation."

The AM and Leviathan decks include debt maturity charts broken out by year as a percentage of portfolio debt.

What ongoing covenants must the borrower maintain under a loan?
"What covenants does the borrower need to maintain under the loan at [property]?"

Covers both financial covenants (debt yield, DSCR, LTV) and operational covenants (insurance, leasing approvals, reporting requirements).

Lease Review
What are the key business terms of a tenant's lease?
"Summarize the key business terms of the lease for [tenant] at [property] — term, rent, options, and any special provisions."

Returns a clean summary from the executed lease document. For tenants with multiple amendments, specify "including all amendments" to get the full current picture.

Does a tenant have an early termination option and what are the requirements?
"Does [tenant] have an early termination option at [property]? What are the notice requirements and any fee obligations?"

Returns the termination effective date, required notice period, conditions (e.g., no default), and any termination fee or penalty payment due.

What are the assignment and subletting provisions in a tenant's lease?
"What are the assignment and subletting provisions in [tenant]'s lease at [property]?"
Are there guarantee or letter of credit requirements in a tenant's lease?
"Are there any personal guarantee or letter of credit requirements in [tenant]'s lease at [property]?"
Amendments
What did the most recent lease amendment change?
"What is the most recent amendment to [tenant]'s lease at [property] and what did it change?"

Amendments are filed alongside lease documents in the Tenants folder. Returns the date, parties, and a summary of all changed terms.

What is the full amendment history for a tenant's lease?
"List all amendments to [tenant]'s lease at [property] in chronological order and summarize each."

Useful for understanding the full evolution of a lease before a negotiation, renewal, or transaction. Returns date, key changes, and parties for each amendment.

Estoppels
Has an estoppel been executed for a tenant and what did it confirm?
"Has an estoppel been executed for [tenant] at [property]? When and what did it confirm?"

Estoppels are filed under the Tenants folder or the Disposition folder for sold properties. Returns the execution date and the key representations made by the tenant.

Loan Documents
What are the recourse provisions in a loan agreement?
"What are the recourse provisions in the loan agreement for [property]?"

Returns whether the loan is recourse or non-recourse, and any carve-outs that create personal liability for the guarantor.

What events of default are defined in a loan agreement and what are the cure periods?
"What events of default are defined in the loan documents for [property] and what cure periods apply?"
What are the prepayment terms for a loan?
"Summarize the prepayment terms for the loan at [property]."

Returns whether voluntary prepayment is permitted, any penalty or yield maintenance provisions, and required notice periods.

What financial reporting must the borrower provide under a loan?
"What are the financial reporting covenants the borrower must satisfy under the loan at [property]?"

Returns the reporting schedule — typically quarterly rent rolls, annual financials, and any lender-specific certificate requirements.
